About Me

Hi! I am Minna, a junior studying Computer Science in the College of Engineering at the Univeristy of Michigan, with a minor in Entrepreneurship and an interest in User Experience Design


As a child, I loved solving riddles. This carried through to my studies, as engineering challenges me to tackle difficult problems and find solutions. I am also a naturally curious person who is always seeking new experiences, yearning to learn new things, and striving to understand people. After travelling all the way to Barcelona, the city of Design, to study Design Thinking, I realized that engineering is about the beauty of creation, but design gives creation a voice. I am passionate about connecting my interest in technology with my passion for design and understanding people.


When I am not working on my projects, I love to travel and feed my wanderlust. I have been to nine countries (ask me about my travel adventures!) and twelve states. You can also find me daydreaming about visiting new places, food, things that have yet to happen, and everything in between!
